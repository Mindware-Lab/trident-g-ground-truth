## Core strategic principle

**iqmindware.com is the priority commercial hub.**

All other IQ Mindware ecosystem channels should either:

```text

2. Capture demand.
3. Demonstrate the protocol.
4. Establish scientific credibility.

6. Route people back to iqmindware.com.
````


The commercial rule is simple:


# Channel 5: eqmindware.com

**Status:** Planned · Launch later

**Positioning:**
Where cognitive resilience meets science. Training reappraisal skills that hold under pressure.

**Job:**
Dedicated brand home for the Resilience Coach product and later resilience / wellbeing / corporate wellness pathways.

**Important architecture update:**

Resilience Coach should not be described as simply:

```text
Z + R
```

The more accurate structure is:

```text
Resilience Coach = Z + G-for-R + R
```

Meaning:

```text
Zone stabilises the state.
G-style reasoning supports variable decomposition, prediction testing and SR-style reappraisal.
R trains resilient reappraisal and recovery under load.
```

The reappraisal component is reasoning-based. It requires the user to separate:

```text
trigger
sensation
emotion
belief
prediction
evidence
action urge
outcome
```

So the resilience protocol includes an embedded G/SR reasoning core.

## Audience

* high-pressure professionals
* students under exam stress
* executives and founders
* coaches
* therapists and wellbeing practitioners
* HR / L&D professionals
* corporate wellbeing teams

## Core content themes — Res, Zone, G-for-R, Long

* Resilience Coach product page
* Zone as readiness gate for resilience work
* appraisal discrimination
* prediction testing
* stress and cognitive performance
* threat-schema updating
* implementation cues
* corporate resilience and performance

## Claim boundary

Frame as:

```text
cognitive resilience and reappraisal practice
```

Do not frame as:

```text
treatment for anxiety, depression, PTSD, chronic pain or diagnosed conditions
```

unless a separate evidence, safety and regulatory pathway exists.

## Funnel role

```text
Middle–bottom.
```

eqmindware.com should probably launch when Resilience Coach has a strong prototype, a clear claim boundary and at least one content sequence ready.

# Channel 6: Brevo Email — Segment Strategy

**Status:** Core · Priority conversion layer

**Overall job:**
Brevo is the owned nurture and conversion layer. It converts website traffic, Substack readers, previous buyers and app users into active users, buyers, paid subscribers and community members.

**Strategic principle:**

```text
Brevo is where intent is organised.
```

## Segment A: Previous buyers — not upgraded

**Priority:** Highest

**Goal:**
Re-engagement → upgrade to cloud IQ Coach / Zone Coach → restore active user status.

These people already crossed the payment threshold. They should be the first commercial campaign.

**Sequence:**

1. “Here is what has changed” — old Trident-G IQ Pro versus new IQ Coach / Zone Coach architecture
2. Profile quiz — re-personalise the relationship
3. Zone Coach introduction — why readiness comes before deeper training
4. Proof and protocol note — how the new system tests transfer
5. Upgrade offer — early adopter rate or bonus paid content
6. Follow-up — “What would make this worth it for you?”

**Cadence:**
5–6 emails over 3 weeks, then monthly if no upgrade.

---

## Segment B: New IQ Coach app buyers

**Goal:**
Successful onboarding → protocol adherence → Zone Coach integration → paid Substack → Discord invite.

**Sequence:**

1. Welcome + protocol orientation
2. How to use Zone Coach before training
3. Which profile are you?
4. Week 2 check-in
5. Paid Substack practical layer offer
6. Discord invite

**Cadence:**
Days 1, 3, 7, 14, 21, 30 post-purchase.

---

## Segment C: New website subscribers

**Goal:**
Build trust → profile identification → IQ Coach conversion → Substack free follow.

**Sequence:**

1. Welcome to IQ Mindware
2. Profile Quiz delivery
3. Five-profile email series
4. Far transfer case essay
5. IQ Coach offer with profile-specific framing
6. Substack free invite

**Cadence:**
Weekly for 6 weeks, then monthly.
